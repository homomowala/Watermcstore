How to Join

Joining FreeWaterMC is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Launch Minecraft (Java Edition or Bedrock Edition).
  2. Go to the Multiplayer section and click "Add Server".
  3. Enter the server address: play.freewatermc.fun
  4. Click "Join Server" and start your adventure!
